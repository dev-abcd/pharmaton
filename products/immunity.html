Pharmaton immunity

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Pharmaton Immunity Ascorbic Acid + Zinc has unique combination of high levels of Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid) and Zinc to strengthen the body's three lines of DEFENSE.

PACK SIZE

100s and 10s

HOW TO USE THE PRODUCT

1 capsule daily or as prescribed by a physician

WARNINGS

If you are allergic to any component in the product.

STORAGE

Store at temperature not exceeding 30c. Protect from heat and moisture. Keep the product out of reach of children.

INGREDIENTS LIST

Ascorbic acid 500mg (as Sodium ascorbate equivalent to 562.43 mg), Zinc 10mg (as Zinc gluconateequivalent to 70.00 mg)

Adult: One capsule daily or as prescribed by a physician.

Q3 When should you not take Abscorbic acid + zinc (Pharmaton Immunity)?
